# 🏠 Mortgage & Housing Platform

## 📋 Overview
Analytics focusing on the mortgage uptake and the affordable housing financing sector in Kenya. Tracks data from KMRC (Kenya Mortgage Refinance Company) and major mortgage players like HF Group.
